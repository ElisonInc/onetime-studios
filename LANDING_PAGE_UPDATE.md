## Design Notes

### Availability Preview Pills
- **Background:** `bg-violet-50`
- **Text:** `text-violet-700`
- **Font size:** `text-xs`
- **Padding:** `px-2 py-1`
- **Border radius:** `rounded`
- **"+X more" pill:** Gray background to indicate additional slots

### Micro-Proof Row
- **Icon:** Green checkmark (`text-emerald-500`)
- **Text:** `text-gray-600`, `text-sm`
- **Spacing:** `gap-6` between items
- **Layout:** Flex wrap, centered

### How It Works Strip
- **Background:** White
- **Borders:** Top and bottom `border-y`
- **Icons:** In colored circles (violet for steps 1-2, emerald for step 3)
- **Arrows:** Hidden on mobile, visible on desktop
- **Padding:** `py-6`
